ABOUT US

Maroyaka began as a simple idea of young entrepreneurs at Pasig City Science High School until it became a dream and a commitment to provide a high standard of food quality and service that can satisfy customers of all ages and from all walks of life. The skills and passion for baking of the shareholders of Sucre Jar Corporation prompted them to open a small business that carefully and delightedly crafts luscious pastries and cakes.

Mission

To satisfy our customer's cravings through safe, appetising, and quality sweet products at reasonable prices.

Vision

To be the reason for our customer's genuine smile every day and everywhere as we deliver through our great-tasting and affordable sweets.
